Hello!

Outside of school I enjoy reading, I am currently reading A Clash of Kings from the A Song of Ice and Fire series, coding small projects, hanging out with friends and playing video games in my free time. I consider coding to be one of the most frustrating and rewarding activities I do! Nothing beats the satisfaction of fixing an error the has eluded you for the past hour.

Of all of the experiences that going to MSU has offered I must say the most rewarding was the study abroad I went on the summer after my sophomore year. I was lucky enough to spend 6 weeks in Greece with an amazing group of 23 students and 3 additional weeks traveling through Italy and Ireland by myself. While traveling I was able to learn a lot about not only the cultures of different countries, but also about myself. If you scroll down you will be able to see a fullscreen picture that I took while inside the Roman Colosseum!
